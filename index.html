WELCOME

Kingsbridge Apartments

Located in Ghana's largest port city, Tema, in the Greater Accra region, and 35 minutes drive from Kotoka International Airport, Kingsbridge Apartments offers airport pick-up & drop off service, and make arrangement for guest who would like to explore and experience the city of Tema, ajoining areas, and beyond. The hotel is also less than 10 minutes drive to two of Ghana's largest shopping mall chain - China mall and Max mart. A stay at Kingsbridge Apartments, accords our guest the opportunity to experience Tema, the city widely known to be at the center of the earth.
