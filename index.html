Why Kingsbridge Royale Apartments?

Spacious Accommodations: Kingsbridge Royale Apartments in Accra offers spacious, adults-only apartments with three bedrooms and four bathrooms. Each unit features a living room, balcony, and garden or city views.
Modern Amenities: Guests enjoy free WiFi, air-conditioning, and a fully equipped kitchenette. Additional amenities include a washing machine, dining area, and soundproofing for a comfortable stay.
Convenient Facilities: The property includes a garden, terrace, and outdoor seating area. Facilities also include a lounge, 24-hour front desk, concierge service, and free on-site private parking.
Prime Location: Located 3.1 mi from Kotoka International Airport, the apartments are near attractions such as Kwame Nkrumah Memorial Park (7.5 mi) and Independence Arch (8.1 mi).
